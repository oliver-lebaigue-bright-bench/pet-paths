# Contributor Code of Conduct

This project follows a simple, clear standard of respectful behaviour.  
All participants—contributors, users, maintainers—must follow this Code of Conduct.

---

## Our Pledge

We are committed to making participation in Pet Paths a harassment-free, safe and welcoming experience.

We do not tolerate:
- Harassment, insults or personal attacks  
- Threatening or aggressive behaviour  
- Doxxing or sharing private information  
- Discriminatory language or behaviour  
- Malicious disruption of conversations or contributions  

---

## Our Standards

Examples of positive behaviour:
- Being respectful and constructive  
- Offering clear and helpful feedback  
- Being open to differing viewpoints  
- Admitting mistakes and correcting them  
- Focusing on what benefits the community  

Examples of unacceptable behaviour:
- Harassment or bullying  
- Public or private threats  
- Posting sexual or violent content  
- Disrespectful or inflammatory comments  
- Conduct inconsistent with project policies  

---

## Enforcement

Maintainers may take any action they deem appropriate, including:
- Removing abusive comments  
- Rejecting contributions  
- Temporary or permanent bans  
- Reporting severe behaviour to platform authorities  

Decisions by project maintainers are final.
